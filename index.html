Our Mission

We want to raise money for NFCC, by doing a bake sale and collecting donations on our website.

How we plan to do it

We will have our bake sale during our school's innovation fair. We will have a menu on our website and at our stand.Once we bake the food at home, we will bring it in to sell. The profits will go to NFCC, a local comunity food bank that helps feed the homeless.
